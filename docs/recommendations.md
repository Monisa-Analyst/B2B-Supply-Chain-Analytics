# Strategic Business Recommendations

Based on the customer and product intelligence generated in this project, the following actions are recommended to optimize sales operations and reduce business risks.

---

## 1. Mitigate Customer Concentration Risk

### Finding
Pareto analysis reveals that the top 10 customer accounts contribute **80.6%** of total revenue, representing a high customer dependency.
### Recommendation
* **Key Account Management**: Establish dedicated key account teams to secure multi-year service contracts and volume commitments from the top 10 accounts.
* **Mid-Market Expansion**: Launch targeted growth campaigns directed at mid-tier accounts to diversify the customer base and reduce dependency on a few dominant corporate buyers.

---

## 2. Optimize Product Portfolio & Inventory

### Finding
ABC product classification shows that 29 Class A SKUs drive **69.4%** of sales, while 161 Class C SKUs drive only **10.1%** of revenue.
### Recommendation
* **Safety Stock Protection**: Prioritize inventory buffer allocations for the 29 Class A items to prevent stockouts of high-velocity components.
* **SKU Rationalization**: Review the bottom 161 Class C items. Standardizing components or phasing out low-demand Class C SKUs will free up warehousing space and reduce inventory holding costs.

---

## 3. Targeted Customer Win-Back Campaigns

### Finding
RFM segmentation identifies 2 key customers in the "At Risk" category and 17 in "Lost Customers".
### Recommendation
* **At-Risk Outreach**: Sales representatives should contact the two at-risk accounts immediately with custom volume incentives to preempt competitor encroachment.
* **Feedback Collection**: Conduct qualitative interviews with inactive "Lost Customers" to understand the reasons for inactivity and address operational gaps.
